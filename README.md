# Preventive Healthcare for Children (Birth–18 Years)

## Overview

Preventive Healthcare for Children is an AI-assisted pediatric health intelligence platform designed to support healthcare professionals, ASHA workers, and parents in monitoring and improving child health outcomes from birth to 18 years of age.

The platform aims to shift healthcare from a reactive model, where interventions occur after disease onset, to a preventive model that identifies risks early and enables timely intervention.

The system combines longitudinal child health records, growth monitoring, immunization tracking, developmental screening, and predictive analytics to provide actionable insights for healthcare stakeholders.

---

# Vision

To build a nationally adaptable Preventive Intelligence Engine for pediatric healthcare that:

* Tracks child health longitudinally
* Identifies health risks early
* Supports clinical decision making
* Empowers parents with preventive guidance
* Strengthens community healthcare delivery
* Generates population-level health insights

Unlike traditional Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems, this platform focuses on prediction, prevention, and continuous monitoring.

---

# Stakeholders

## 1. Doctors / Pediatricians

Doctors receive AI-assisted decision support through a centralized dashboard.

Features:

* Child health profile
* Growth trend visualization
* Immunization history
* Developmental milestone tracking
* Risk prediction alerts
* Follow-up recommendations

Example AI Output:

"Child has a 70% probability of developing malnutrition within the next 3 months."

The final diagnosis and treatment decisions remain the responsibility of the healthcare professional.

---

## 2. ASHA Workers

ASHA workers act as field data collectors.

Responsibilities:

* Household visits
* Height measurement
* Weight measurement
* Vaccination status collection
* Basic health data collection

Features:

* Child registration
* Data entry dashboard
* High-risk child list
* Follow-up visit scheduling
* Community health tracking

Collected data is synchronized with the central healthcare platform.

---

## 3. Parents / Caregivers

Parents receive preventive health guidance through a mobile application.

Features:

* Growth charts
* Vaccination reminders
* Developmental milestone checklists
* Health recommendations
* Follow-up notifications
* Health history records

Example Notification:

"MMR vaccination is due in 5 days."

## AI Malnutrition Risk Prediction

Primary Research Objective:

Predict the likelihood of a child becoming malnourished within a future time window.

Potential Inputs:

* Age
* Height
* Weight
* Growth history
* Vaccination history
* Illness records
* Demographic indicators

Outputs:

* Risk score
* Risk category
* Contributing factors
* Follow-up recommendations

Example:

Risk Score: 70%

Prediction:

High probability of malnutrition within the next 3 months.
